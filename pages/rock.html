ABOUT ME MY WORK WHY ROCK

Why Rock?

I find rock cycle and design to be pretty much similar to each other.
Both have an infinite cycle of breaking down and building up.

Igneous Rock

Formed from cooling magma — raw beginnings, creation, energy.

Sedimentary Rock

Layered through time — patience, history, memory of the earth.

Metamorphic Rock

Transformed by heat and pressure — resilience, change, growth.
